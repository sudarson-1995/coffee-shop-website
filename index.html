ABOUT US

At Grand Coffee House, tradition meets emotion in every sip. Born in 2010, Grand Coffee House has blossomed into a chain of more than 120 stores, spreading the aroma of authentic South Indian filter coffee throughout India.

OUR GALLERY

We believe that a cup of filter coffee is not just a beverage; it's a story passed down from generation to generation. With every brewing ritual, we infuse the richness of Madras heritage, captivating your senses and transporting you to the bustling streets of our beloved city. Join us on this enchanting journey as we take the essence of Madras coffee to the world.
